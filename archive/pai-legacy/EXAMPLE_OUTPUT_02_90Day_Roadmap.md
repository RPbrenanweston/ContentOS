# The 90-Day Roadmap: Sentinel

**Prepared for:** Sentinel Security
**Duration:** 90 days
**Objective:** Establish foundation, implement strategy, and drive measurable results

---

## Overview

This roadmap outlines our phased approach to transforming Sentinel's digital positioning and buyer journey. We've tailored each phase to your mission of fortifying enterprise defenses and the needs of security leaders (CISOs, IT executives) who demand proof, not promises.

---

## Month 1 (Days 1-30): Foundation & Discovery

**Theme:** Understanding Your Competitive Position & Building Strategy

### Key Activities

1. **Competitive Landscape Deep Dive**
   - Audit 8-10 competing security consulting firms' positioning, messaging, and buyer journey
   - Identify market gaps where Sentinel has unique advantage (incident response speed? Threat modeling proprietary framework? Board-level advisory?)
   - Document what's working in security services marketing and what's commoditized

2. **Customer Research Synthesis**
   - Conduct 5-7 interviews with past clients and prospects (C-suite, security practitioners) to understand buying triggers
   - Map the actual decision journey: What made them hire Sentinel? What almost stopped them?
   - Identify messaging that resonates vs. generic security firm language

3. **Website Content Audit**
   - Catalog every page, service description, case study, and piece of messaging
   - Identify inconsistencies in tone, value props, and buyer-centric language
   - Create gap analysis: where is Sentinel *under*-communicating strength?

### Deliverables

- Competitive positioning matrix (what makes Sentinel different?)
- Customer research summary with key insights
- Messaging framework draft (value propositions for 3 buyer personas)
- Website content audit with recommendations

### Success Metrics

- Strategy approved by Sentinel leadership
- 5+ customer insights documented and actionable
- Clear differentiation narrative identified

---

## Month 2 (Days 31-60): Strategy & Build

**Theme:** New Messaging, Buyer Journey, Updated Architecture

### Key Activities

1. **Messaging & Positioning System**
   - Develop 3 buyer-persona-specific messaging tracks (CISO, IT Director, Board)
   - Create tagline and 30-second value prop that beats current homepage
   - Write positioning statement that guides all future content

2. **Homepage & Key Landing Pages Redesign**
   - New homepage structure: Problem → Sentinel's Approach → Proof → Next Steps
   - Redesign service pages to lead with business outcomes (not feature lists)
   - Build case study pages with ROI language and quantified impact

3. **Content Strategy & Roadmap**
   - Identify 8-10 research topics where Sentinel has proprietary insights
   - Plan quarterly thought-leadership content (blogs, white papers, video interviews)
   - Build content calendar for Month 3-6

4. **Design Direction & Visual System**
   - Refresh visual identity (modern, enterprise-confident, not trendy)
   - Establish component library for responsive, consistent UX
   - Create design system documentation for future scaling

### Deliverables

- Messaging framework and persona-specific value propositions
- New homepage wireframe and copy
- 3-5 redesigned service pages
- 6-month content roadmap with topics and formats
- Visual design system and updated brand guidelines

### Success Metrics

- Messaging approved and team trained on new narrative
- Homepage and service pages live and measured
- Content roadmap locked and assignment clear
- Design system adopted for future updates

---

## Month 3 (Days 61-90): Scale & Optimize

**Theme:** Launch, Measure, Iterate, and Establish Momentum

### Key Activities

1. **Full Website Launch**
   - Deploy redesigned homepage, service pages, and content hub
   - Migrate case studies to new structure with ROI language
   - Set up analytics to track engagement, conversion, and user behavior

2. **Thought-Leadership Engine Kickoff**
   - Publish first research piece / whitepaper
   - Launch blog with 2-3 initial posts establishing unique perspective
   - Begin quarterly webinar / speaker series planning

3. **Sales Enablement & Messaging Rollout**
   - Train sales team on new positioning and messaging
   - Create one-sheets for competitive situations
   - Build deck templates that lead with Sentinel's differentiation

4. **Performance Analysis & Optimization**
   - Track key metrics: homepage bounce rate, service page conversion, content engagement
   - A/B test messaging and CTA placement
   - Identify highest-performing content topics for Month 4+ focus

### Deliverables

- Live redesigned website with new messaging
- First published research / thought-leadership piece
- Sales enablement deck and one-sheet templates
- 30-60-90 performance dashboard showing engagement and conversion lift

### Success Metrics

- Website traffic from target personas increasing
- Service page conversion rate improves by 20%+
- Thought-leadership content gets meaningful engagement (shares, backlinks)
- Sales team reports improved buyer positioning conversations
- First customer attribution to new messaging

## How We'll Measure Success

- **Monthly Check-ins** to review progress against milestones
- **Clear KPIs for each phase**: positioning clarity, design completion, messaging adoption, conversion lift
- **Adaptive Approach**: Roadmap adjusts based on learnings (e.g., if a particular message resonates, we double down)

## Beyond 90 Days

- Quarterly thought-leadership publishing (maintaining authority and SEO)
- Continuous A/B testing and optimization of buyer journey
- Expansion to vertical-specific messaging (e.g., financial services security vs. healthcare compliance)
- Building Sentinel's speaking platform and industry recognition
