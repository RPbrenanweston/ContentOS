## FAQ Section
**Headline:** How it works in practice

**4 Questions:**

**Q: Do we need our own ATS?**
A: No. We can set up a lightweight system (like Notion or Airtable) or integrate with your existing tools.

**Q: Who writes the outreach emails?**
A: We do. We use your brand voice and role requirements to craft sequences. You review and approve before we send.

**Q: What is the 75th percentile rule?**
A: For senior roles, we only advance candidates who score in the top 25% of the candidate pool based on your scorecards. This filters out noise before you spend time interviewing.

**Q: Can we pause if we're not hiring?**
A: Yes. Let us know and we'll pause operations. Your contract term adjusts accordingly.

## FAQ Section

**6 Questions:**

**Q: Are there setup fees?**
A: No. Setup is included in your first month.

**Q: Do you charge placement fees?**
A: No. Our pricing is flat monthly. No percentage of salary, no per-hire fees.

**Q: What happens if we don't hire anyone in a month?**

**Q: Do you offer discounts for annual prepayment?**
A: The 12-month commitment is already discounted compared to month-to-month. We don't offer additional discounts for prepayment.
