## Our Core Principles

**1. Radical Transparency Over Comfort**
We believe in telling you what we actually think, even when it's not what you want to hear. If a messaging direction isn't working, we'll say so. If we see a market opportunity you're missing, we'll surface it. You hired us for strategic clarity, not agreement.

**2. Evidence Before Opinions**
Nothing we recommend comes without data or customer research to back it. When we suggest a new homepage structure, we'll show you competitive benchmarks and user behavior from your target personas. No hunches. No "it's best practice." Proof.

**3. Your Team's Success Is Ours**
We're not here to build something impressive and leave. We're building *with* you—training your team, documenting systems, ensuring that when this engagement ends, you own the capability and momentum we've created together.

**4. Speed Without Sacrifice**
We move fast because security firms don't have time to wait. But "fast" never means "sloppy." Every deliverable is polished, strategic, and launch-ready. We compress timelines without cutting quality.

## How We Communicate

**Weekly Syncs (45 minutes, Tuesdays 10am)**
- Progress on current phase milestones
- Any blockers and how we're solving them
- Decisions needed from your side
- Preview of next week's deliverables

**Monthly Strategy Reviews (90 minutes, first Thursday)**
- Deep review of performance metrics and learnings
- Competitive intelligence and market shifts
- Course corrections to the 90-day roadmap (if data suggests it)
- Alignment on Month 2/3 priorities

**Quarterly Vision Sessions (half-day, off-site)**
- Broader strategic planning
- Capability building and team development
- Planning for 180-day and 1-year success
- Investment decisions for the next phase

## Response Times & Availability

- **Urgent (strategy decision blocks our work)**: 24 hours
- **Standard requests (feedback, approvals, decisions)**: 48 hours
- **Office hours**: Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm ET (we're available for quick questions outside these windows, but reserve deep work time)
- **After-hours & weekends**: We don't work nights/weekends unless it's a pre-planned push

## How We Handle Disagreements

Disagreements are healthy. Here's how we resolve them:

1. **State the facts** — What data are we disagreeing on?
2. **Acknowledge both perspectives** — What's the concern on each side?
3. **Run the experiment** — If it's about messaging or design, we test it. A/B testing beats opinions.
4. **Decide and commit** — Once we decide, we commit fully. No second-guessing mid-execution.

If we fundamentally disagree on strategy, we'll escalate to leadership (your CEO, our leadership) rather than letting it fester.

## Escalation & Issue Resolution

**Minor adjustments** (design tweaks, copy edits)
- Handled in weekly syncs

**Scope adjustments** (new deliverables, timeline shifts)
- Documented, agreed in writing, reflected in timeline and budget

**Strategic concerns** (we think the approach isn't working)
- Raised immediately with data/evidence
- Discussed in next available sync or emergency call
- Escalated if not resolved within one week

We're here to tell you hard truths early, not surprises late.
