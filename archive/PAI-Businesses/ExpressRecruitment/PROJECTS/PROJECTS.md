# Express Recruitment — Projects

**Business Context:** Embedded recruitment solution on subscription model for businesses under 100 staff. Uses human-in-the-loop processes augmented by AI and workflow optimization. Solves: Senior leaders/founders stuck doing recruitment without dedicated HR/recruiter resources. Alternative to expensive dedicated recruiters or high-cost agencies.

**Stage:** Development (product being built)
**Revenue Model:** Subscription (monthly/annual recurring)
