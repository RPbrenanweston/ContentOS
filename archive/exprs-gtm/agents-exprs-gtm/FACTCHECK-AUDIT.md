## Risk Assessment: What Could Backfire

**🟡 MODERATE RISK:** The 70-85% "structurally repeatable" claim

**Why:** It's consultant synthesis, not a published figure. If a prospect asks "where's that from?", the answer is "That's our analysis based on maturity research," not "CIPD says so."

**Mitigation:** In CRO conversations, frame as "Based on patterns we see in..." rather than "Research shows..." This is more honest and builds trust.

**Recommendation:** Consider adding a footnote in the Surgical vs. Systemic whitepaper:
> "The 70-85% estimate is derived from talent acquisition maturity research (Bersin, McKinsey) combined with our analysis of typical company hiring patterns. Your organization may vary."

This turns a potential weakness into transparency.
