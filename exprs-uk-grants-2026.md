### 2. R&D Tax Credits (RDEC Scheme)

**Funding**: 20% credit rate = ~15% net benefit on qualifying R&D spend
**Type**: Retrospective cash credit via HMRC
**Match Funding**: N/A (claimed after expenditure)
**Application**: Annual tax return via specialist advisor

**Exprs Eligibility**: ✅ **HIGH PROBABILITY** (if developing novel AI)

**What Qualifies**:
- ✅ Developing new ML algorithms for candidate matching
- ✅ Creating novel data pipelines for recruitment workflow automation
- ✅ Building custom AI integrations that advance state-of-the-art
- ✅ Training models for constrained/specialized recruitment domains
- ✅ Research into fairness, bias detection, explainability in recruitment AI

**What DOES NOT Qualify**:
- ❌ Using existing pre-trained LLMs without novel development
- ❌ Routine data preparation using standard tools
- ❌ Basic system deployment and infrastructure setup
- ❌ Connecting existing AI APIs without advancing underlying technology

**Critical Insight**: HMRC's bar is rising rapidly. Simply using AI ≠ R&D. You must be advancing AI technology itself, not just applying existing tools. **Documentation must be meticulous** — HMRC actively monitors AI-generated claims.

**Application Complexity**: MEDIUM-HIGH (requires specialist R&D tax advisor)

**⚡ ACTION**: Engage specialist (GrantTree, Alexander Clifford, or equivalent) to assess current AI development work for R&D qualification. **Start documenting qualifying activities NOW** even if not claiming until year-end.

## Key Insights

### The Product Advantage
Exprs qualifies strongly for most innovation grants because SaaS/product innovation maps cleanly to Innovate UK eligibility criteria requiring TRL progression and demonstrable IP. This is a significant advantage over pure service/consultancy businesses.

### The AI Bar Is Rising
HMRC and grant assessors are increasingly sophisticated about AI. Using LLMs ≠ R&D. You must demonstrate genuine technological advancement, not just application of existing tools. **Documentation and specificity are critical**.

### The Strategic Play
The AI Assurance Innovation Fund (Spring 2026) is the highest-value strategic opportunity. It positions Exprs not just as "another AI recruitment tool" but as a leader in responsible, fair, transparent AI deployment — a differentiator that compounds in value.

### The Compound Effect
Grant success builds on itself:
1. Small grant (£25K-£50K Growth Catalyst) → establishes Innovate UK track record
2. R&D tax credits → funds match-funding requirements for larger grants
3. Larger grant (£100K-£2M Smart Grants) → credibility for Innovation Loans
4. Innovation Loans (£100K-£5M) → scale-up capital

Each win makes the next application stronger.
