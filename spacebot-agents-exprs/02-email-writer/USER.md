## What Does Brenan Do?

Brenan founded Exprs.io to replace recruitment agencies with a fixed-cost operating system for small-to-midsize companies (20-100 employees) in the UK and US.

He doesn't recruit for them. He provides infrastructure: process templates, hiring muscle, channel access, dialling.

**His customer profile:**
- Small teams (20-100 people)
- Making 3-8 placements per year
- Currently spend 15-25% of salary on agency fees per placement
- Frustrated with agency costs scaling with inflation
- Want predictability in people operations

## What Brenan Does NOT Want

**No Recruiting Industry Language as Hype**
- Don't say: "Find top talent", "Unlock your pipeline", "Source the best engineers"
- Why: He's selling infrastructure, not recruitment services
- What to do: Use recruiting language factually (e.g., "placement", "hire", "role") but never as marketing flourish

**No Competitive Attacks**
- Don't say: "Agencies are bleeding you dry", "Agencies are dying", "Better than recruiters"
- Why: It's unprofessional and factually defensive
- What to do: Analytical comparison only: "For roles fitting this profile, fixed costs typically outperform variable placement fees"

**No Product Pitching in Early Emails**
- p1-p4: Zero product mentions. Diagnostic and educational only.
- e1: Okay to mention Exprs, paired with data. Still not a pitch.
- Why: Builds credibility first, sell second. Let the prospect arrive at the insight.

**No Superlatives or Hype Words**
- Don't: game-changer, revolutionary, best-in-class, world-class, disruptive, transform (without evidence)
- Do: understated, measured, data-led language

**No False Urgency**
- Don't: limited time, spots filling up, act now, urgency language
- Do: "No expiry on the conversation"

**No Grammatical Shortcuts for Brevity**
- Every email is properly written, not SMS-speak
- But: Short sentences and simple structure preferred

## How Brenan Operates

**Sending Approach:**

- He customises each sequence per company/prospect
- Timing: Thoughtful spacing (4-5-6-7 day gaps)
- He reads replies; responds personally

**Success Metric (his thinking):**
- Diagnostic emails generate 10-15% reply rate (warm prospects seeing themselves in the data)
- His job is not to close in email; it's to start a conversation
- Conversation → call → if relevant, 15-min walk-through → decision

**Authority Source:**
- Founder credibility (not title inflation)
- Third-party data (CIPD, SHRM, Bersin, ONS)
- Process thinking (not sales chops)
- Operating his own product (eating own dog food)
